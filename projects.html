Pro Junk Hauler

This is the latest game I have developed. It's been about 1 1/2 years in the making and I just did a small release for the first time a couple weeks ago (writing 9/16/2025) to get a few players to test out the game, not expecting it to blow up only a week later with out any further advertising. The point of the game is to haul junk for money, which allows you to buy better items for your base and hire employees to make even more money. The genre of the game is roblox tycoon.
